LobbyDirect

A Citizen Advocacy Platform for 21st Century Politics. Contact your Senators and Congressmen directly!
LobbyDirect is a citizen advocacy platform
that allows you to communicate directly with multiple government
and media leaders simultaneously
Lobby Direct identifies Senators, Congressional and State representatives automatically from you zipcode!
Send Twitter, TruthSocial, Email & Webpage messages automatically!
Customize your memo and directly communicate your opinions!
How it works?
Find out more about the capabilities of Lobby Direct.us
Hot Topics

“Hot Topics” are added daily from headlines of the day

Persons involved in the Topic are identified by Lobby Direct

Click on the internet icon to load the primary news source

Favorites

Lobby Direct will automatically list your Senators, Governor and Congressional Representative in your favorites list

Choose from > 10,000 contacts to add to your favorites list

Website Automatic Pre-Fill

Government officials make it onerous and difficult to communicate with them

Find the proper contact WebPages automatically and Pre-Fill your information

Your message is automatically entered

Click the submit button and you’re done!
